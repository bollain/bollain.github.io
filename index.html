Born and raised in Mexico, but proud to call Vancouver and Canada my home. I love everything about this city, its vibrant beer culture, close proximity to the great outdoors, and its budding technology industry.

I have a background in Finance and Economics, and spent over five years working in corporate lending and underwriting with some of the largest players in Canada and the world. Though I made some great friendships along the way, I found that my heart was not in it for the long run, which led to one of the toughest choices in my life.

I am now in my second and final year of the Bachelor of Computer Science at the University of British Columbia, where I have found my true calling as a computer scientist. I love programming and problem solving; there is nothing more satisfying than turning an idea into a finished product.

In my free time I enjoy cycling, rock climbing in Squamish, brewing beer, and hanging out with my feline friend Miguelito. On a rainy Vancouver afternoon you can probably find me wrangling code at a local coffee shop.
